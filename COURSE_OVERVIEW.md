# How to Build a Client-Booking Website That Accepts Payments (Without a Developer)

## Course Overview

Welcome to the complete step-by-step guide to building your own professional client-booking website with payment processing capabilities. This course will teach you how to create a fully functional booking system similar to popular platforms, but customized for your business.

### What You'll Build

By the end of this course, you'll have a complete booking website that includes:

- ✅ **Online Booking System** - Clients can book appointments 24/7
- ✅ **Payment Processing** - Accept deposits and full payments online
- ✅ **Calendar Integration** - Automatic calendar management
- ✅ **Client Accounts** - Customer registration and booking history
- ✅ **Email Notifications** - Automated confirmations and reminders
- ✅ **Admin Dashboard** - Manage bookings, clients, and services
- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- ✅ **Professional UI** - Modern, beautiful interface

### Who This Course Is For

- Business owners who want to accept online bookings
- Service providers (beauty salons, consultants, coaches, therapists, etc.)
- Entrepreneurs looking to automate their booking process
- Anyone who wants to learn modern web development
- Complete beginners (no coding experience required!)

### Prerequisites

**You don't need any coding experience!** However, you should have:

- A computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux)
- Basic computer skills (using a web browser, installing software)
- An internet connection
- A willingness to learn and follow instructions step-by-step
- About 2-3 hours per module (the course is self-paced)
