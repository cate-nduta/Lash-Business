# Lesson 10.1: How Online Payments Work

**Estimated Time**: 25 minutes

## Introduction

Understanding how online payments work is essential before integrating payment processing into your website. This lesson explains the payment flow, what payment gateways are, and how money moves from your customer's account to yours.

**What You'll Learn:**
- What payment processing is
- How payment gateways work
- The payment flow from customer to merchant
- Different payment methods
- Fees and transaction costs

---

## What Is Payment Processing?

### Simple Explanation

**Payment processing is:**
- How customers pay you online
- The system that handles transactions
- Secure transfer of money
- From customer to your account

**Think of it like:**
- Cash register for online business
- Secure way to accept payments
- Automated money transfer
- Professional payment system

**Payment processing = Accepting money online!**

---

## The Payment Flow

### Step-by-Step Process

**1. Customer initiates payment:**
- Clicks "Pay" button
- Enters payment details
- Confirms purchase

**2. Payment gateway processes:**
- Validates payment info
- Checks funds available
- Secures transaction
- Processes payment

**3. Bank/processor handles:**
- Transfers money
- Verifies transaction
- Processes payment
- Confirms completion

**4. Money arrives:**
- Funds in your account
- Transaction confirmed
- Customer notified
- Booking confirmed

**Simple: Customer → Gateway → Bank → Your Account!**

---

## What Is a Payment Gateway?

### The Middleman

**Payment gateway:**
- Connects your website to banks
- Processes transactions securely
- Handles payment details
- Protects sensitive information

**Examples:**
- Stripe
- PayPal
- Pesapal
- Square

**Think of it as:**
- Secure bridge between customer and you
- Handles all payment complexity
- Keeps you safe from fraud
- Makes payments easy

---

## How Payment Gateways Work

### The Process

**1. Customer enters details:**
- Card number
- Expiry date
- CVV code
- Billing address

**2. Gateway encrypts data:**
- Secures information
- Protects from hackers
- Encrypts transmission
- Safe transfer

**3. Gateway sends to bank:**
- Validates with bank
- Checks funds
- Authorizes payment
- Processes transaction

**4. Gateway returns result:**
- Success or failure
- Transaction ID
- Confirmation
- Your website notified

## Transaction Fees

### Understanding Costs

**Payment gateways charge fees:**
- Per transaction fee
- Percentage of amount
- Fixed fee per transaction
- Monthly fees (sometimes)

**Typical fees:**
- **Stripe:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **PayPal:** 2.9% + fixed fee
- **Pesapal:** Varies by region
- **Square:** Similar to Stripe

**Example:**
- $100 transaction
- 2.9% = $2.90
- + $0.30 = $3.20 total fee
- You receive: $96.80

## Common Questions

### "Do I need a merchant account?"

**Answer:** No! Payment gateways like Stripe and PayPal handle everything. You just connect your bank account to receive funds.

---

### "How long until I receive money?"

**Answer:** Usually 1-3 business days. Some gateways offer instant payouts for a fee.

---

### "Is it safe?"

**Answer:** Yes! Payment gateways are highly secure and PCI compliant. They handle all sensitive data.

---

### "What if a payment fails?"

**Answer:** The gateway will notify you and the customer. The customer can try again with a different payment method.

---

## Key Takeaways

3. **Multiple payment methods available** - Cards, digital wallets, bank transfers
