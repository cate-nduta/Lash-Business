# Lesson 8.3: Google Gmail Option

**Estimated Time**: 25 minutes

---

## Introduction

Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) offers professional email through Gmail with your custom domain. While it's a paid service, it provides powerful features and integration with Google's suite of tools. This lesson covers setting up professional email with Google Workspace as an alternative to Zoho Mail.

**What You'll Learn:**
- What Google Workspace is
- How it differs from personal Gmail
- How to set up Google Workspace
- Pricing and features
- When to choose Google over Zoho

---

## What Is Google Workspace?

### Gmail for Business

**Google Workspace:**
- Professional Gmail with your domain
- Uses familiar Gmail interface
- Includes Google tools (Drive, Calendar, etc.)
- Paid service (~$6/month per user)
- More features than free options

**Example:**
- Personal Gmail: `yourname@gmail.com`
- Google Workspace: `hello@yourbusiness.com`

**Same Gmail interface, professional domain!**

---

## Google Workspace vs. Personal Gmail

### Key Differences

**Personal Gmail:**
- Free
- `@gmail.com` domain
- Personal use
- Limited business features

**Google Workspace:**
- Paid (~$6/month)
- Your custom domain
- Business features
- Professional tools

**Both use Gmail interface, but Workspace is for business!**

## Setting Up Google Workspace

### Step 1: Sign Up

**1. Go to Google Workspace:**
- Visit `workspace.google.com`
- Click "Get Started"

**2. Choose plan:**
- Business Starter (~$6/month)
- Business Standard (~$12/month)
- Business Plus (~$18/month)

**3. Start free trial:**
- 14-day free trial
- No credit card required initially
- Test before committing

---

### Step 2: Add Your Domain

**1. Enter domain:**
- Type your domain (e.g., `yourbusiness.com`)
- Click "Continue"

**2. Verify ownership:**
- Google will verify you own domain
- Add DNS record or HTML file
- Follow instructions

**3. Complete verification:**
- Wait for verification
- Usually takes a few minutes

---

### Step 3: Create Email Address

**1. Create user:**
- Enter first user email (e.g., `hello@yourbusiness.com`)
- Set password
- Add user details

**2. Complete setup:**
- Finish account setup
- Access Gmail

**3. Start using:**
- Log into Gmail
- Use familiar Gmail interface
- Professional email address

---

### Step 4: Configure DNS

**1. Get DNS records:**
- Google provides DNS records
- MX records for email
- Other records as needed

**2. Add to domain:**
- Log into domain registrar
- Add DNS records Google provides
- Wait for propagation

**3. Verify:**
- Google verifies automatically
- Email starts working
- Usually within hours

---

### Web Access

## Real-World Example

### Setting Up for Lash Studio
