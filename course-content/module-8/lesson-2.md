## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Create Zoho Account

**1. Go to Zoho Mail:**
- Visit `mail.zoho.com`
- Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"

**2. Choose plan:**
- Select "Free" plan (up to 5 users)
- Or choose paid plan if needed

**3. Create account:**
- Enter your email (can use personal email for signup)
- Create password
- Verify email address

---

### Step 2: Add Your Domain

**1. Access domain settings:**
- Log into Zoho Mail
- Go to "Control Panel" or "Domain Settings"
- Click "Add Domain"

**2. Enter your domain:**
- Type your domain (e.g., `yourbusiness.com`)
- Click "Add Domain"

**3. Verify domain ownership:**
- Zoho will ask you to verify you own the domain
- Choose verification method (usually DNS record)

---

### Step 3: Configure DNS Settings

**What DNS settings do:**
- Tell email servers where to send emails
- Connect your domain to Zoho Mail
- Required for email to work

**Where to configure:**
- Your domain registrar (where you bought domain)
- Or your hosting provider
- DNS management panel

**DNS records needed:**
- MX records (mail exchange)
- TXT records (verification)
- CNAME records (if needed)

---

### Step 4: Add DNS Records

**1. Get DNS records from Zoho:**
- Zoho will show you required DNS records
- Copy the values provided

**2. Add to your domain:**
- Log into domain registrar/hosting
- Go to DNS management
- Add the records Zoho provided

**3. Wait for propagation:**
- DNS changes take time (usually 15 minutes to 48 hours)
- Zoho will verify automatically

---

### Step 5: Create Email Addresses

**1. Go to user management:**
- In Zoho Mail control panel
- Click "Users" or "Email Accounts"

**2. Create email address:**
- Click "Add User" or "Create Email"
- Choose email address (e.g., `hello@yourbusiness.com`)
- Set password
- Create account

**3. Repeat for additional addresses:**
- Create as many as needed (up to 5 on free plan)
- `info@yourbusiness.com`
- `bookings@yourbusiness.com`
- etc.

## Common Setup Issues

### Issue 1: DNS Not Propagated

**Problem:**
- Email not working
- DNS changes not active yet

**Solution:**
- Wait 15 minutes to 48 hours
- Check DNS propagation status
- Verify records are correct

---

### Issue 2: Wrong DNS Records

**Problem:**
- Email not receiving/sending
- DNS records incorrect

**Solution:**
- Double-check DNS records
- Ensure exact values from Zoho
- Verify record types (MX, TXT, etc.)

---

### Issue 3: Verification Failed

**Problem:**
- Domain verification not working
- Can't verify ownership

**Solution:**
- Check DNS records are added
- Wait for DNS propagation
- Try alternative verification method
