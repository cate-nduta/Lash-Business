# Buying Your Domain: Step-by-Step Guide

## Introduction

Your domain name is your website's address on the internet (like lashdiary.co.ke). This lesson will walk you through buying a domain name step-by-step. It's easier than you think, and we'll use real examples from popular domain registrars.

## What is a Domain Name?

**Think of it like this:**
- Your website files live on a server (like a computer)
- That server has an IP address (like 192.168.1.1)
- A domain name (like lashdiary.co.ke) is a friendly name that points to that IP
- Instead of typing numbers, people type your domain name

**Examples:**
- `google.com` - Google's domain
- `lashdiary.co.ke` - LashDiary's domain
- `yourbusiness.com` - Your future domain!

2. **Click "Add to Cart"** next to your chosen domain
3. **Review the price**

### Domain Extensions Explained:

- **.com** - Most popular, $10-15/year
- **.co.ke** - Kenya, $20-30/year
- **.com.ng** - Nigeria, $20-30/year
- **.co.za** - South Africa, $20-30/year
- **.org** - Organizations, $10-15/year

**Tip:** .com is usually best if available, but country extensions work great for local businesses.

## Common Questions

### Q: How long does it take?

**A:** Domain purchase is instant! You own it immediately after payment.

### Q: Can I change my domain name later?

**A:** You can't change it, but you can buy a new one and redirect the old one. It's better to choose carefully.

### Q: What if my domain is taken?

**A:** Try:
- Different extensions (.co.ke instead of .com)
- Add your location (yourname-nairobi.com)
- Use your full name
- Add "studio" or "services"

### Q: How much does it cost?

**A:** 
- .com: $10-15/year
- Country extensions: $20-30/year
- Some premium domains cost more

### Q: Do I need to renew?

**A:** Yes, domains are rented yearly. Enable auto-renewal so you don't lose it!
